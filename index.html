welcome to byhand.website

an open guide to hand coded websites

This site comes out of my own interest in building and browsing hand coded websites and my teaching since the early days of the pandemic which has focused largely on web based projects. Rather than being a refined course, I see byhand.website as offering a starting point for artists, designers, and anyone else who is interested in building their own weird and wonderful corner of the internet. Whether it's a portfolio page, a net art, or anything else you can think of, I hope that byhand.website helps you find joy and excitement in hand coding websites.

- Chelsea Thompto
